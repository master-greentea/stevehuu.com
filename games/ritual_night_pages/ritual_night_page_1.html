Overview

Ritual Night is a multiplayer social deduction game with no eliminations (until the very end). It has been a side project my friends and I worked on since our second year in university, and we continued to work on it full-time for an entire year after graduation.

The entire project is completed in-house, from all the design, art, sound, code, network, to even our albeit very limited marketing. We created our own studio Outfox Games in senior year and shipped Ritual Night under its name.
