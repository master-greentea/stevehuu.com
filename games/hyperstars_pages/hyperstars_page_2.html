Technical Art

I was tasked with many different aspects of special effects in the game. The goal of them is to juice up the game as much as possible. For that a lot of efforts were put into the impact frames and character effects.

The effects in the game are divided as follows:

  • Modular Impact (Hit) Effects
  • Character Effects
  • Environment Effects
  • UI Animations

Note that many of these clips are taken straight out of what I've shown the team during production, so they come from different stages of development. Have fun!
