Voice "Acting" Sound Effects

I created all of the sound effects in Hoho Game's debut game: Thought Experiment Simulator.

The pillar behind TES' sound design is cartoony fun. Inspired by this amazing series by a Chinese animation studio as reference. I made all the sound effects in the game with just my voice.

Sound effects in progress

In total, there were more than 300 individual clips of sound effects all recorded, edited, packed, and implemented by me. Many of which had undergone a few iterations as a result of playtesting and direct feedback from the creator himself.

Here is a select few of the most unhinged ones without context for your auditory pleasure.
